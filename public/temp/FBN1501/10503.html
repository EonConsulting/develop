Question 6

Suppose \(p\) is a real number.  Consider the following statements:

A:  If \(p\leq0\), then \(-4p\leq0\).
B:  On a number line, \(p\) and \(-p\) are the same distance away from \(0\).

Which of the following is true?

  1. Only A is correct.
  2. Only B is correct.
  3. Both A and B are correct.
  4. Neither A nor B is correct.

  1. \(56\)
