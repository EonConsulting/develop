Properties

The properties of square roots are:

 

\(\sqrt{\PalAa a\PalAb b}\)

\(=\)

\(\sqrt{\PalAa a}\times\sqrt{\PalAb b}\)

for \(\PalAa a\geq0\) and \(\PalAb b \geq0\).
 

\(\displaystyle\frac{\sqrt{\PalAa a}}{\sqrt{\PalAb b}}\)

\(=\)

\(\sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{\PalAa a}{\PalAb b}}\)

for \(\PalAa a\geq0\) and \(\PalAb b >0\).
 

\(\sqrt{\PalAa a}\times\sqrt{\PalAa a}\)

\(=\)

\(\PalAa a\)

  

 

The property \(\sqrt{\PalAa a\PalAb b}=\sqrt{\PalAa a} \times \sqrt{\PalAb b}\) only applies to numbers in roots that are multiplied.

Remember that \(\sqrt{\PalAa a+\PalAb b}\neq\sqrt{\PalAa a}+\sqrt{\PalAb b}\)  and \(\sqrt{\PalAa a-\PalAb b}\neq\sqrt{\PalAa a}-\sqrt{\PalAb b}\).
