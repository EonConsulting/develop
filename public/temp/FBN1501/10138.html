With and without brackets

Without brackets \(\PalAa{8-2}+3\) \(=\) \(\PalAa{6}+3\) \(=\) \(9\)
With brackets \(8-(\PalAb{2+3})\) \(=\) \(8-\PalAb{5}\) \(=\) \(3\)

In the example without any brackets, the problem is solved by working from left to right and performing all the addition and subtraction.

When brackets are used, you first perform the operations inside the brackets, so you will get a different answer.

Therefore, if you want a specific order of performing operations you must enforce it using brackets.
