Add and subtract 2

If a number has no sign it usually means that it is a positive number: \(\PalAb5\) is really \(\PalAb{+5}\).

Addition

  • Adding a positive number is just simple addition:

\(\PalAb+\text{ and }\PalAb+\text{ gives }\PalAb+\)

\(\begin{align} ({+5})\PalAb+(\PalAb{+3})&=({+8})&\text{or}\\ 5\PalAb{+3}&=8 \end{align}\)

  • Subtracting a negative number is the same as addition:

\(\PalAa-\text{ and }\PalAa-\text{ gives }\PalAb+\)

\(\begin{align} ({+5})\PalAa-(\PalAa{-3})&=({+8})&\text{or}\\ 5\PalAb{+3}&=8 \end{align}\)

Subtraction

  • Subtracting a positive number is just simple subtraction:

\(\PalAa-\text{ and }\PalAb+\text{ gives }\PalAa-\)

\(\begin{align} ({+5})\PalAa-(\PalAb{+3})&=({+2})&\text{or}\\ 5\PalAa{-3}&=2 \end{align}\)

  • Adding a negative number is the same as subtraction:

\(\PalAb+\text{ and }\PalAa-\text{ gives }\PalAa-\)

\(\begin{align} ({+5})\PalAb+(\PalAa{-3})&=({+2})&\text{or}\\ 5\PalAa{-3}&=2 \end{align}\)
