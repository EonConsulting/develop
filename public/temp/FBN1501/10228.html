Properties summary

Below is a list of the properties of exponents:

 

\(a^{\PalAa m}\times a^{\PalAb n}\)

\(=\)

\(a^{(\PalAa m+\PalAb n)}\)

\(=\)

\(a^{\PalAa m+\PalAb n}\)
 

\(\left(a^\PalAa m\right)^\PalAb n\)

\(=\)

\(a^{(\PalAa m\times\PalAb n)}\)

\(=\)

\(a^{\PalAa m\PalAb n}\)
 

\((\PalAa a\PalAb b)^n\)

\(=\)

\(\PalAa a^n\PalAb b^n\)

 

 
 

\(\left( \displaystyle\frac{\PalAa a}{\PalAb b}\right)^n\)

\(=\)

\(\displaystyle\frac{\PalAa a^n}{\PalAb b^n}\)

    
 

\(\displaystyle\frac{x^\PalAa a}{x^\PalAb b}\)

\(=\)

\(x^{\PalAa a-\PalAb b}\)

    
 

\(a^{\PalAb{-n}}\)

\(\displaystyle\frac{\PalAb1}{a^\PalAb n}\),

 \(a\neq0\)

\(\displaystyle\frac{y^\PalAb{b}}{x^\PalAa a}\),

\(x\neq0 \), \(y\ne0\)
 

\(a^\PalAa0\)

\(\PalAa1\),
