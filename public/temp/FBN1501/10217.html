The histogram is one of the most common ways to visually represent data.  A histogram is a graphical presentation of a frequency table.

A frequency table is an organised count of items or values.  The raw data are organised into intervals and counted to show how many of the raw data lie in each interval.

These tables are often useful for discovering basic statistical information about a data set.
