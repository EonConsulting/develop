Question 3

  1. \(\displaystyle\frac{x}{yz}\)
     
  2. \(\displaystyle\frac{yz}{x}\)
     
  3. \(\displaystyle\frac{y}{xz}\)
     
  4. \(\displaystyle\frac{xz}{y}\)
