Question 7

The employees of two different companies planned separate trips to a convention.
The employees of Company A rented and filled one minibus and six buses with \(372\) employees.  Company B rented and filled four minibuses and twelve buses with \(780\) employees.  Each minibus and each bus can carry the same number of employees.

Let \(x\) represent the number of employees that a minibus can carry.
Let \(y\) represent the number of employees that a bus can carry.

Two equations that can be solved to determine the number of employees each vehicle can carry is:

  1. \(x+6y=372\) and \(4x+12y=780\)
  2. \(6x+y=372\) and \(12x+4y=780\)
  3. \(x+6y=780\) and \(4x+12y=372\)
  4. \(x+4y=372\) and \(6x+12y=780\)
  5. None of the above
