Introduction

Profit is a very important concept for any business.  Profit earned by a business can be used to measure the success of the business.  Profit is also an important signal to other providers of finance to a business.  Banks, suppliers and other lenders are more likely to provide finance to a business that can demonstrate it makes a profit and can pay debts as they fall due.

Profits earned which are kept in the business are an important source of finance for the business.  The moment a product is sold for more than it cost to produce, then a profit is earned which can be reinvested.

Profit can be measured and calculated.  So here is the formula:

profit \(=\) total revenue \(-\) total costs

Profit maximization is the process by which a firm determines the price and output level that returns the greatest profit.
