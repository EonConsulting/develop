Question 1

The expression

\(25-(4-2\times 8)+16 \div 2 \times 4\)

can be rewritten as:

  1. \(25+12+2\)
  2. \(25-16+32\)
  3. \(25+12+32\)
  4. \(25-12+2\)

Question 2

Consider the following equations:

\(\begin{align} \text {A:}&&21-3\times 2 &= 36\\ \text {B:}&&36\div3\times2 &=6 \end{align}\)

Which of the following is true?

  1. Only A is correct.
  2. Only B is correct.
  3. Both A and B are correct.
  4. Neither A nor B is correct.
