Subtract exponents

If the numerator and denominator of a fraction are powers
with the same base you subtract the exponents.

\(\displaystyle\frac{x^\PalAa a}{x^\PalAb b}=x^{\PalAa a-\PalAb b}\)

Example

\(\begin{align}\displaystyle\frac{x}{x^\PalAb 5}&=x^{\PalAa1-\PalAb 5}\\ &=x^{-4}\\ &=\displaystyle\frac{1}{x^4} \end{align}\)
