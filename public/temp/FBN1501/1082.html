Descriptive statistics

Two main statistical methodologies are used in data analysis, namely descriptive statistics and inferential statistics.

Descriptive statistics refer to collecting, summarising and processing data to transform the data into information.  Data can be collected through surveys, summarised via methods like calculating the sample mean, and processed by presenting it in tables and graphs.

Inferential statistics provide the basis for predictions, forecasts and estimates that are used to transform data into knowledge.

This learning unit will focus on descriptive statistics.
