A formula for calculating the future value of annuity

We have seen that if \(R\) is the payment in rand made at each payment interval in respect of an annuity at interest rate \(i\) per payment interval, then the amount or future value of the annuity after \(n\) intervals is given by the formula:

\(S=R\left[\displaystyle\frac{(1+i)^n-1}{i}\right]\)

where \(S\) is the future value of the annuity
  \(R\) is the regular payment
  \(i\) is the interest rate per payment interval
  \(n\) is the number of payment intervals
