Identity properties

Four other fundamental rules that you must take notice of are:

The product of any number with zero is zero:            \(A\times\PalAa0=\PalAa0\)

The product of any number with one is the number:  \(A\times\PalAb1=A\)

The sum of any number and its negative is zero:     \(A+-A=\PalAa0\)

The product of any number and its inverse is one:  \(A\times A^{-1}=\PalAb1\)
