Examples

Sometimes it is easier to break up a difficult multiplication:

Example: What is \(5 \times 206\)?

\(\begin{align} 5 \times 206 &= 5 \times (200 +6) \\ &=5 \times 200 + 5 \times 6\\ &=1000 + 30 \\ &=1030 \end{align} \)

Or it is easier to combineÙÄÄù:

Example: What is \(13 \times 7 + 13 \times 3\)?

\(\begin{align} 13 \times 7 + 13 \times 3 &= 13 \times (7+3) \\ &=13 \times 10\\ &=130 \end{align} \)

You can use it with subtraction too:

Example: What is \(36 \times 8 -34 \times 8\)?

\(\begin{align} 36 \times 8 -34 \times 8 &= (36-34) \times 8 \\ &=2 \times 8\\ &=16 \end{align} \)
