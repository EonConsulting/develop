System of inequalities with no solution

Find the solution of the following system of inequalities:

\(\begin{align} \PalAa{x+y}&\PalAa \leq \PalAa{-3}\\ \PalAb{x+y} &\PalAb \geq \PalAb 3 \end{align}\)

Drawing the lines and their solution spaces gives:

There is no place where the individual solution spaces overlap.  The lines \(\PalAa{x+y=-3}\) and \(\PalAb{x+y=3}\) never intersect because they are parallel lines with different \(y\)-intercepts.

Since there is no intersection, there is no solution.
