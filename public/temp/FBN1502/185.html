Exercise instructions

Do an exercise to test your knowledge on systems of linear inequalities in two variables.  Make sure you understand the calculations before you proceed to the next learning unit.  This exercise does not contribute towards your semester mark or final mark. It is for exercise purposes only.

Instructions

  • Fill in the correct answer for each question.
  • After completing all the questions, click the 'Check answers' button to obtain your score.
  • Aim to achieve at least \(60\%\) for the exercise before looking at the solutions.
  • If you scored less than \(60\%\), click the 'Clear' button to clear your previous selections.
  • Answer the questions again and click the 'Check answers' button.
  • If you scored \(60\%\) or more, click the 'Show calculations' button to see the solutions.
  • When you are done, click the 'Continue' button to access the assignment questions.
  • After completing the assignment questions, proceed to the next learning unit.
