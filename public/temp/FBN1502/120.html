Calculate \(S\): An example

Calculate the accumulated sum on \(\Cur500\Cdp00\) invested for \(10\) years at \(7\frac{1}{2}\%\) per annum and compounded annually.

Write down all the information given:

Principal \((P)\)

\(\text{principal}=\Cur500\Cdp00\)

\(P=\Cur500\Cdp00\)

Term \((n)\)

\(\text{term}=10\:\text{years}\)

\(n=10\)

Interest rate \((i)\)

\(\text{interest rate}=7\frac{1}{2}\%\:\text{compound annually}\)

\(i=0\Ndp075\)

 

Use the general situation formula to calculate the accumulated sum \((S)\):

Formula

\(S\)

\(\)\(=\)

\(P(1+i)^n\)

Substitute

 

\(=\)

\(\Cur500\Cdp00(1+0\Ndp075)^{10}\)

Calculate

 

\(=\)

\(\Cur500\Cdp00\times1\Ndp075^{10}\)

Simplify

 

\(=\)

\(\Cur1\Tsp030\Cdp52\)

The accumulated sum/future value is \(\Cur1\Tsp030\Cdp52\).
