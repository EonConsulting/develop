Drawing the graph on a set of axes with two points

Only two points are needed to determine an equation of a straight line and to draw the graph of a straight line.

If you are not convinced of this, just mark two points on a piece of paper and try to put more than one straight line through them.

To draw a straight line we make use of the general method.

General method to draw a straight line

Step 1 Draw your axes and label them.
Step 2 Choose the scale of the axes.
Step 3 Plot the two given points.
Step 4 Draw a line through the two plotted points.

Example

Graph the straight line that passes through the points \((1;4)\) and \((4;2)\).
