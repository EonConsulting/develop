Part to part

A part to part ratio means one part of a ratio is compared to another part.

The trick with ratios is to always multiply or divide the numbers by the same value.

Example  \(\)

The numbers of the ratio \(\PalAa3\) \(:\) \(\PalAb4\)  
multiplied by the same value \(2\) \(\PalBc{\times2}\)   \(\PalBc{\times2}\)  
scales the ratio up to \(\PalAa6\) \(:\) \(\PalAb8\)  

So, \(3:4\) is the same as \(3\PalBc{\times2}:4\PalBc{\times2}=6:8\).
