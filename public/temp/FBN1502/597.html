Question 4

The derivative of the function

\(f(x)=2x^{-\frac{1}{3}}+5\)

is:

  1. \(f'(x)=-\displaystyle\frac{2}{3}x^{\frac{2}{3}}\)
  2. \(f'(x)=\displaystyle\frac{5}{3}x^{-\frac{4}{3}}\)
  3. \(f'(x)=-\displaystyle\frac{2}{3}x^{-\frac{4}{3}}\)
  4. \(f'{x}=-\displaystyle\frac{2}{3}x^{-\frac{4}{3}}+5\)
  5. None of the above

Question 6

\(C'(x)=25-0\Ndp10x\)

where \(x\) represents the number of jackets made.  Find \(C'(70)\) and interpret the result.

  1. When \(70\) jackets are made, it will cost \(\Cur18\Cdp00\) to produce the \(71^{\text{st}}\) jacket.
  2. It will cost \(\Cur18\Cdp00\) to produce \(70\) jackets.
  3. When \(18\) jackets are made, it will cost \(\Cur70\Cdp00\) to produce one additional jacket.
  4. It will cost \(\Cur70\Cdp00\) to produce \(18\) jackets.
  5. None of the above.
