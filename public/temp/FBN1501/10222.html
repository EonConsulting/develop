Same power

The power of a fraction property allows us to separate or combine the numerator and denominator of a fraction if they are both raised to the same power.

\(\left( \displaystyle\frac{\PalAa a}{\PalAb b}\right)^n=\displaystyle\frac{\PalAa a^n}{\PalAb b^n}\)

Example

\(\begin{align} \left(\displaystyle\frac{\PalAa{xy^2}}{\PalAb{z^3}}\right)^4&=\displaystyle\frac{\PalAa x^4\left(\PalAa {y^2}\right)^4}{\left(\PalAb {z^3} \right)^4}\\ &=\displaystyle\frac{\PalAa x^4\PalAa y^8}{\PalAb z^{12}} \end{align}\)
