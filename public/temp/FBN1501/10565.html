Question 4

Sipho Galane, a concerned citizen, has decided to conduct a study of the voters in his town to determine if they approve of the way the mayor is running the council.  He intends to ask \(300\) citizens of voting age their views in order to obtain an approval rating (a percentage) that will be published in the local newspaper.  Sipho comes from a large town.  The town clerk, who monitors the voting booths, informed him that all the voters in the entire town registered at a recent election and \(310\Tsp000\) voters were counted.  Consider the following statements:

A:  The \(310\Tsp000\) registered voters in the town form the population.
B:  The sample unit is the vote of a particular citizen.
C:  The group of \(300\) citizens that Sipho Galane will interview form the sample.
D:  The variable that will be studied, is a particular citizen.

Which of the following is true?

  1. Only A, B and C
  2. Only A, B and D
  3. Only A and C
  4. Only C
