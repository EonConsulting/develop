How is the GDP growth rate measured?

Statistics SA publishes different approaches to measure the growth of real GDP, according to the publication http://www.statssa.gov.za/articles/15%20Measuring%20GDP.pdf.  

These approaches include:

  1. The annual real growth rate
  2. The real GDP quarterly growth at a seasonally adjusted and annualised rate (Q/Q rate)
  3. The real GDP unadjusted year-on-year quarterly growth (Y/Y growth)
