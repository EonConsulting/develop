Instructions Page

Welcome to FBN1501. The study material consists of three topics. Each topic is divided into learning units.

Start studying from the beginning of Topic 1 (Numbers and working with numbers), Learning unit 1 (Priorities) and do not jump around between the learning units.

The first time you work through a learning unit, you will find that all the pages are locked. The pages will become unlocked as you progress. You can then access any of the unlocked pages by clicking on the page name in the navigation panel on the left side of the screen.

When you have completed a page, click the “Proceed” button  to access the next content page.

When completing activities and exercises, please take note of the following:

  • Do not use any form of thousands separator,
    • for example 10 000 should be typed as 10000.
  • For decimal values, use a comma “,” separator,
    • for example, type 15,60 and not 15.60.
  • Decimal accuracy will be indicated if not clear.
  • Currency will always be to two decimal places if a decimal value is required.
