Period and payments

A mortgage of \(\Cur 200 \Tsp 000 \Cdp 00\) has to be amortised in monthly payments at an interest rate of \(6\%\) per year, compounded monthly.  Four periods for repaying the loan were considered: \(10\), \(15\), \(20\) and \(25\) years.

The table below shows how much interest you would have to pay depending on the monthly payment and amortisation period chosen.

  \(15\)     \(\Cur 1 \Tsp 687 \Cdp 71\)     \(\Cur 303 \Tsp 788 \Cdp 46\)     \(\Cur 103 \Tsp 788 \Cdp 46\)
