Like terms

Powers can only be added or subtracted if they
have the same base and the same exponent.

\(3\PalAa a^\PalAb n+2\PalAa a^\PalAb n=5\PalAa a^\PalAb n\)   and   \(3\PalAa a^\PalAb n-2\PalAa a^\PalAb n=\PalAa a^\PalAb n\)

Examples

\(\begin{align} 6\PalAa y^\PalAb3+2\PalAa y^\PalAb3&=8\PalAa y^\PalAb3&&\text{and}\\ 6\PalAa y^\PalAb3-2\PalAa y^\PalAb3&=4\PalAa y^\PalAb3\end{align}\)
