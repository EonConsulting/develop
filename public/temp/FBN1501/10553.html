Question 7

A woman’s salary for the years 2009, 2011 and 2013 as well as the CPI for these years are given in the table below.  The base year is 2009 with a CPI of \(100\).

Year Yearly salary in rands CPI
2009 \(159\Tsp820\) \(100\)
2011 \(171\Tsp850\) \(99\Ndp7\)
2013 \(182\Tsp820\) \(102\Ndp1\)

Consider the statements below.

A: The difference between her nominal salary of 2011 and 2009 is less than the difference between her real salary of 2011 and 2009.

B: The difference between her nominal salary of 2011 and 2009 is greater than the difference between her real salary of 2011 and 2009.

C: The difference between her nominal salary of 2013 and 2009 is less than the difference between her real salary of 2013 and 2009.

Which of the following is true?

  1. Only A.
  2. Only B.
  3. Only C.
  4. Only B and C.
