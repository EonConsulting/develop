Examples

Equation   Solution or root
\(2x+3\) \(=\) \(x+4\)   \(x\) \(=\) \(1\)
\(A+1\) \(=\) \(3\)   \(A\) \(=\) \(2\)

In each case the values on the right (the solutions) make the statements on the left (the equations) true.

The process of determining which values of the variables make the statement true is known as solving the equation.
