Compound interest amount

Suppose Paul invests \(\Cur600\Cdp00\) for three years at a compounded interest rate of \(10\%\) per annum.

We can see that for compounded interest the interest amount increases every year; it is different every year.

Compounded interest can be applied in both the following situations:

  • Make one investment and leave it to gain an increased amount of interest per year.
  • Borrow one amount of money which accumulates an increased amount of interest per year.
