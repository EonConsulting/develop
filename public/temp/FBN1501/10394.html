Definition of an equivalent fraction

There are many ways to write a fraction.  Fractions that
represent the same number are called equivalent fractions.

Example

\(\displaystyle\frac{2}{4}\) and \(\displaystyle\frac{4}{8}\) are equivalent fractions.

Equivalent fractions are basically the same as equivalent ratios.  To find out if two fractions are equivalent, use a calculator and divide.  If the answer is the same, then they are equivalent.
