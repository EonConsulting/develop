Summary

Profit

  • Profit is a very important concept for any business.

profit \(=\) total revenue \(-\) total cost

Marginal profit

  • Marginal profit is the profit that would be made by producing and selling one additional unit of a product or service. Therefore, the formula for the extra unit can be written as:

marginal profit \(=\) marginal revenue \(-\) marginal cost

Marginal profit, marginal revenue and marginal cost

  • If marginal revenue \(>\) marginal cost, then marginal profit is positive.
  • If marginal revenue \(<\) marginal cost, then marginal profit is negative.
  • If marginal revenue \(=\) marginal cost, then marginal profit is zero.

Profit maximisation

  • Profit maximization is the process by which a firm determines the price and output level that returns the greatest profit.
     
  • To find the maximum profit, let the marginal profit equal zero, that is \(P'(x)=0\), and solve for \(x\).  At the \(x\)-value where \(P'(x)=0\), profit is maximised.
