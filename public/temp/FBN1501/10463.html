Question 2

Consider the following equations:

A:  \(\sqrt[3]{a^2b} \times \sqrt[3]{ab^2}=ab\)

B:  \(\sqrt[3]{a^3b^2}-\sqrt[3]{a^3b}=a\sqrt[3]{b^2-b}\)

Which of the following is true?

  1. Only A is correct.
  2. Only B is correct.
  3. Both A and B are correct.
  4. Neither A nor B is correct.
