Variable on right side

If you need to solve an inequality, but the variable is on the wrong side of the inequality sign, what do you do?

Example

Solve \(10>m+3\).

Solve the inequality

\(10\)

\(\PalAa>\)

\(m+3\)

Add \(-3\) both sides

\(10-3\)

\(\PalAa>\)

\(m+3-3\)

 

\(7\)

\(\PalAa>\)

\(m\)

Rewrite this inequality

\(m\)

\(\PalAb<\)

\(7\)

Reason

If \(b\) is greater than \(a\), then \(a\) is less than \(b\).

If the variable is on the wrong side of the inequality,
swop the sides and reverse the inequality sign.
